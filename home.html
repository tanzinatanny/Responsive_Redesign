Website to Redesign

A local pizza store in Hamilton, NY, which is very popular and the only pizza store in a large area. Their website is akaslices


Problems

Usability
  • Ambiguous business name, solved by redesigning the banner and logo.
  • Visual noise and low-quiality images, solved by creating a centered carousel with higher quality images.
Learnability
  • Confusing Navbar that scrolls away, solved by sticky navbar.
  • Unclear Call to action button, solved by clear CTA buttons.
Memorability
  • Social Media Icons not taking to their social media pages, solved by clearly grouping them linking actual pages.
  • Location map is just an image, does not take to the actual location address, solved by linking google maps in button.

Accessibility

WAVE API could be used by the business for integrating better accessibility
